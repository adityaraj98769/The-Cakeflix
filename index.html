Our Story

Made with Heart,
Delivered with Love

The Cakeflix was born from one simple belief: every celebration deserves a cake as unique as the person being celebrated. Based in the heart of Gomti Nagar, Lucknow, we are a home bakery crafted with passion and an obsession for detail.

Every cake is handmade — from the sponge to the last fondant flower — using only fresh, quality ingredients. We don't believe in factory shortcuts or frozen bases. What you get is entirely bespoke, made to order, and made to amaze.

We're Lucknow's sweetest secret, and we'd love to be part of your story.
